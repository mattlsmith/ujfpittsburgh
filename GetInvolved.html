Get Involved

Volunteer

The Jewish community needs your help, and Federation offers several ways to give your time:

Join Jewish Federation’s Volunteer Center for a group volunteer opportunity

Let us connect you with one of our beneficiary agencies

 

Become a Fundraiser

Connect with friends and the community by raising money for Federation’s Annual Campaign and the Jewish Community Foundation. Contact Becca Hurowitz at 412-992-5226 or bhurowitz@jfedpgh.org to learn more.

 

Attend Events

From Jewish learning to notable speakers to social events, Federation provides opportunities to connect, learn and grow.

Check out the Community Calendar for upcoming events.

 

Donate

Your gift to Federation’s Annual Campaign or Jewish Community Foundation helps Pittsburghers and Jews around the world.

Learn more about ways to give.

 

Advocate for Israel

Federation provides a voice for Israel in Pittsburgh and beyond through government and community advocacy.

Learn more about our Community Relations Council.

 

Go on a Mission

Travel to exciting locations with other Pittsburgh Jews. Learn about our heritage and how Federation helps Jewish communities worldwide while experiencing luxury accommodations and great sightseeing.

Learn more about travel opportunities.

 

Get Involved with YAD

The Young Adult Division has so many ways of connecting young people (early 20's through early 40's) with the community - learn more here.
