Spring Event 2016

Featuring
Sharon Tal
Head Designer of Maskit,
Israel's Premier Fashion House

Celebrating Generations of Empowerment

Wednesday
April 6, 2016
7:00 p.m.
Rodef Shalom Congregation

Inspiring 34-year-old designer Sharon Tal, formerly of Lanvin and Alexander McQueen, will share her journey revitalizing Israel’s premier fashion and decorative art house, Maskit. Entrepreneur Ruth Dayan, the widow of Israeli general and politician Moshe Dayan, pioneered Maskit in 1954, allowing her to create jobs for new immigrants while also preserving the crafts and culture of Israeli communities. The brand, described as luxury ready-to-wear fashion, skyrocketed to international recognition in the 70’s and 80’s. Maskit closed in 1994 until Sharon Tal joined in 2011. Today, at 99 years old, Dayan is still working with Tal to re-establish the brand and continue its legacy. This is an amazing story of women’s empowerment between generations.

Following Tal's talk, enjoy a dessert reception and trunk show featuring pieces from Maskit's latest collection - 5% of proceeds from all purchases will be donated to the Federation.
