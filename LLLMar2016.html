Join us for our next Lion of Judah Lunch and Learn
Evening Edition!

Tuesday, March 15, 2016

PLEASE NOTE NEW TIME!
6:00 - 8:00 PM

Held at the home of Marsha Marcus

Address provided upon RSVP

Join us for the second of three opportunities to take part in a thought-provoking discussion on Ethics Today presented by Rabbi Dr. Danny Schiff. This will be an evening of learning, noshing, and schmoozing as we discuss The Death of Privacy. Privacy has diminished drastically in the twenty-first century - is losing privacy a significant loss, and if so, what should we do about it?

 

*Heavy hors d'oeuvres will be served - dietary laws observed*
*This is a non-solicitation event*

This event is for women who are Lions of Judah (women making a personal commitment to the Annual Campaign of $5,000 or more) or Lion of Judah Endowment (LOJE) participants (women who have established an endowment fund of $100,000 or more in their name), or whose household commitment to the Jewish Federation's Annual Campaign is $10,000 or more.
