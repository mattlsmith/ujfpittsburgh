The Jewish Community Foundation & The Jewish Healthcare Foundation present

MEDICAL ETHICS IN JEWISH LAW
with Foundation Scholar
RABBI DR. DANNY SCHIFF

In this 3-part series, Rabbi Schiff will explore Jewish insights relating to some of the more challenging ethical issues that emerge within contemporary healthcare.  Rabbi Schiff will present compelling Jewish sources from ancient times through the twenty first century that will illuminate Jewish thinking on these difficult questions.  In each session, Rabbi Schiff will discuss a different issue, presenting a wonderful opportunity to engage in Jewish learning with a one-of-a-kind scholar.

  • Wednesday, February 17: END OF LIFE IN JEWISH LAW
  • Wednesday, March 16: ABORTION IN JEWISH LAW
  • Wednesday, May 25: TRIAGE - JEWISH LAW ON WHO SHALL LIVE WHEN NOT ALL CAN
