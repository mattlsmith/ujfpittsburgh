Looking to find or plan an event far into the future? The "Date" drop down menu only displays the next 6 months, but you can access as far into the future as you need. Click the drop-down menu for the month displayed, and choose the last month available to you. Now click the drop-down again, and you will see 6 more months into the future. Repeat as needed. If you have any questions about the community calendar, please contact us at info@jfedpgh.org.
