Bridges: Jewish Women's Professional Series

A new initiative designed for working women in the Pittsburgh Jewish community, Bridges: Jewish Women's Professional Series convenes Jewish professional women in the Greater Pittsburgh area through networking and educational events, with the goal of fostering professional relationships.

Throughout the year, we will plan unique one-hour lunches to discuss important topics facing female professionals today.

In 2016 gender is still very much a "hot button" issue in the workplace. Join us for a lively panel discussion where we will hear from women who have successfully navigated male-dominated industries. Our guest panelists include Lori Shure, Senior Vice President of Treasury Management at PNC Financial Services Group, Inc., Susan Gromis Kaplan, Partner at Marcus & Shapira, LLP, and Robbin Steif, Owner and CEO of LunaMetrics. Whether there's a gender gap at your workplace or not, you'll be sure to learn great tips for success.

Successfully Navigating Male-Dominated Industries in the 21st Century

Thursday, March 31, 2016

12:00-1:00pm

The Tower at PNC Plaza
300 Fifth Avenue
DC/Delaware Room, 16th Floor
Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Cost: $10 per person*

 

*Lunch will be served - dietary laws observed. This is a non-solicitation event.

In order to attend Bridges events, Jewish female professionals must have demonstrated a commitment to the community through a contribution to the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's 2016 Annual Campaign. If you have not already done so, please donate online here or contact us at 412.992.5227 or kcohen@jfedpgh.org.

Bridges: Jewish Women's Professional Series is a program of Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's Women's Philanthropy.
