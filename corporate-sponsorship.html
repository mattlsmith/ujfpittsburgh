Corporate Sponsorship

About

Jewish Federation's Annual Campaign, Foundation and Corporate Giving programs provide a complete network of health, education and social services to those in need in the Pittsburgh region. The Jewish Federation serves approximately 60,000 people annually in and around Pittsburgh. We offer many levels of sponsorship with various benefits, or we can customize a sponsorship package that meets your company's philanthropic goals.

Why Give?

Corporate sponsors receive broad exposure to a large and valued customer base of Jewish philanthropists, business people and community leaders all while showing the companies' commitment to strengthening the Pittsburgh community.

How to Become a Corporate Sponsor

Corporations and businesses can become donors in one of several ways: 

  • By contributing to the Jewish Federation’s Annual Campaign
  • By sponsoring programs or events


Contributions to the Annual Campaign and event and program sponsorship support a wide variety of social services that help people in need. These services are available to the entire Pittsburgh community regardless of religion.


Corporate giving benefits businesses by increasing public awareness of their corporate philanthropy. 

To find out more about the Jewish Federation’s corporate giving program, please contact Sally Stein at 412.992.5243 or sstein@jfedpgh.org. 

Contact

For more information on the Corporate Sponsorship Program, please contact Sally Stein at sstein@jfedpgh.org or 412.992.5243.
