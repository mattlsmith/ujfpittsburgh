What Is Partnership 2Gether?

Partnership 2Gether (P2G) is a program of the Jewish Agency for Israel (JAFI) that:

  • Pairs the Pittsburgh Jewish community with the city of Karmiel and the adjoining region of Misgav in the Central Galilee region of Israel
  • Provides opportunities for 500 Jewish communities worldwide to participate in cultural, educational and social exchanges with Israeli communities
  • Offers Diaspora Jews a hands-on vehicle to assist in Israel’s economic and community development

How Does P2G Work?

  • A Joint Steering Committee comprised of professional and lay leadership from Pittsburgh, Karmiel, and Misgav meets twice a year to plan and discuss new and existing programs, and to approve the budget. Project liaisons and program area chairs meet throughout the year to oversee implementation of programs and maintain relationships with local agency partners.
