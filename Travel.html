Missions and Travel

Jewish Federation offers our donors luxury travel to a variety of destinations. We arrange everything and connect you with both cultural and Jewish sightseeing. Travelers commit to a minimum gift to the Federation’s Annual Campaign. Learn more about opportunities available at different giving levels. 

Beyond the Casbah


Mission to Morocco
March 11 - 20, 2018
Explore the exotic cities and rich history of Morocco in this exclusive mission experience. Meet the last remaining Berber Jews in the remote village of Ourika Valley. Enjoy glamorous adventures during the day and luxury accommodations at night. 

Learn more...

Summer Mission to Israel


National Young Leadership Mission
July 3 - 9, 2017
 Deepen your connection to Israel and its people on National Young Leadership’s Summer Mission to Israel. Experience tikkun olam (repairing the world) through the extraordinary work of Federation and its partners. And open your eyes to how you too can make an impact. It’s a trip with purpose—a mission.

Learn more...

 

General Assembly 2017


Los Angeles, CA
November 12 - 14, 2017
On this community mission to Israel, select your own tracks from specialty areas such as outdoor adventure, culinary & wine, and politics & democracy, and tour the highlights of Israel in Jerusalem, Tel Aviv and Karmiel/Misgav.  

Learn more...

 

Heart to Heart Mission to Israel 2018


A Woman's Journey to Israel
February 4 - 8, 2018
Embark on a life-changing odyssey. Join like-minded, smart, motivated women from across North America on a uniquely inspiring journey. Spend 5 amazing days traveling Israel. Explore the country's rich culture, embrace your spirituality and see how Federation helps those most at risk.
