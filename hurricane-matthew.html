Emergency Relief for Victims of Hurricane Matthew

With winds topping 140 miles per hour, Hurricane Matthew has created a path of destruction. The storm has wreaked havoc and forced hundreds of thousands to flee for their lives. The full scope of the storm’s devastation is still being assessed.

Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh is joining Jewish Federations across North America to mobilize humanitarian support for those areas most hard hit by Matthew. The power of the Jewish Federations of North America network means that you can provide relief to workers already on the ground in affected areas—often within 24 hours of the disaster. One hundred percent of your donation will go to the Hurricane Matthew Relief Fund.

Please consider a donation below.


 

Emergency Relief for Victims of Hurricane Matthew

The emergency campaign for victims of flooding in West Virginia has ended. Thanks to the generosity of our community, we raised over $1000 for relief!
