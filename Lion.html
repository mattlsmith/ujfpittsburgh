Lion of Judah

The Lion of Judah is a symbol of today's Jewish woman's strength, her commitment to the organized Jewish world, and her financial contribution of at least $5,000 to the Annual Campaign. Hundreds of women in Pittsburgh have joined more than 17,500 women world-wide as Lions of Judah.

The Lions of Judah are the most dynamic philanthropic Jewish women in the world.  Representing all ages and backgrounds, Lions are an international sisterhood of global activists who care deeply about the Jewish future.  

Lions play a vital role in creating social justice, aiding the vulnerable, preserving human dignity and building Jewish identity.  The sisterhood sets an exemplary standard of leadership and giving.  

The Lion of Judah Endowment (LOJE) is a key part of the Lion of Judah program. Learn more about creating a LOJE to honor your commitment to Jewish life in perpetuity.
