Twice the

United Jewish Federation
2007 Annual Community Campaign
and Israel Emergency Campaign
Solicitor Training

...Twice the
Challenge


As the crisis in Israel evolves, emergency needs continue to grow... and we are faced with a dual challenge. We must assure that both emergency and day-to-day needs are met - in Israel, here at home and in Jewish communities around the world. We must strengthen the Annual Community Campaign and the Israel Emergency Campaign.

Solicitor Training is more critical than ever, as we address...

Making a Dual Ask
