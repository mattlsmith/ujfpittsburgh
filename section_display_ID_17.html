Missions
Home > Missions



An Israel Mission can mean so many things to so many people — a dream vacation for some, a spiritual awakening for others. A call to adventure that must be answered, or a shared experience that can become the foundation for the rest of your life — and for those you love. So pack your bags — you're going home!  Read more about missions.

Upcoming Missions to Ethiopia

Based on the extraordinary success of the two previous Operation Promise missions to Ethiopia and Israel, UJC has scheduled three additional missions for all $10,000+ donors to the federation campaign, and Local Operation Promise Campaign Chairs.

  • January 14 - 19, Israel/Ethiopia ($10K minimum gift)
  • April 15 - 20, Israel/Ethiopia ($10K minimum gift)

    • The first two missions were oversold and from now on, each mission will be on a ‘first come, first served’ basis. When we reach our capacity we will close out the mission. Contact Michelle Cheslow at michelle.cheslow@ujc.org or (212) 284-6711. 


    Security on Trips to Israel
    Your participation on a UJC mission at this critical time reaffirms your commitment to and support for Israel. You bring with you a powerful message of unity and solidarity, along with the unwavering spirit of peace and tikkun olam that is part of every Jew and so vital for Israel Now. We thank you for travelling with us and want you to feel confident that your mission experience will be exciting, meaningful, memorable and safe.   More about security when travelling in Israel.
