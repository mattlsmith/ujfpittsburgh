Jewish Community Foundation

About

The Jewish Community Foundation helps donors give back to our community by establishing philanthropic funds, endowments, trusts, and bequests that address our most pressing needs, enrich our culture, and strengthen our community. The Foundation enables donors to create an enduring legacy that honors and reaffirms our traditions and shapes and assures a vibrant future for what we treasure most - families, our community and our Jewish heritage.

Click here to learn more about the Jewish Community Foundation's investment performance.

Centennial Fund for a Jewish Future (CFJF)

The centerpiece of the Foundation is the Centennial Fund for a Jewish Future (CFJF), an endowment that will generate funds to build and sustain a vibrant and dynamic Pittsburgh Jewish community. The Fund for a Jewish Future will be transformational in funding exciting formal and experiential Jewish learning opportunities to engage the young and old, affiliated and unaffiliated, in meaningful Jewish living.
