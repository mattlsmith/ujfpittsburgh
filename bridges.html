Bridges: Jewish Women's Professional Network

Bridges was launched in 2015 as a new program of the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's Women's Philanthropy. The series serves as a platform for Jewish female professionals in the Greater Pittsburgh area to convene through networking and educational events, with the goal of fostering professional relationships. Throughout the year, we will plan unique one-hour lunches to discuss important topics facing female professionals today as well as networking happy hours.

In order to attend Bridges events, Jewish female professionals must have demonstrated a commitment to the community through a contribution to the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's 2016 Annual Campaign. If you have not already done so, please donate online here or contact us at 412.992.5227 or rlipkin@jfedpgh.org.
