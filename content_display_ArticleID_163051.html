Mission Atidim Itinerary



This is a tentative itineraty for the mission.  It is subject to change. 

Please continue to check back, as details will continue to change and to be added.

 

Tuesday, February 28, 2006
You may choose any domestic flight that you would like from Pittsburgh to Newark but you MUST arrive at Newark no later than NOON.
Many of us will be traveling on Continental flight 2346 from Pittsburgh to Newark at 9:15 am.
Please call Richelle Kurtis at 412.992.5204 when you have booked your domestic flight.

3:50pm - Depart Newark on Continental Flight 84 (overnight flight)

Wednesday, March 1, 2006
10am - Arrive in Israel.  Israelis from our partnership region, Karmiel / Misgav (the Region), will welcome Mission participants at the airport.  Participants will recieve name tags and bus assignments.
1pm-2pm - Shechianu in Misgav (Kamun).  Introduction to the Region from Misgav Mayor Erez Kriezler.  Bake Pitot, press olives to make oil and learn to play the Bedouin drum.
2pm-3pm - Lunch with the steering committee and greetings from Karmiel mayor Adi Eldar.
3:30pm-5pm - Visit the Children's Village of Karmiel.  Tour and meet with children in group homes.  Hand out backpacks.  Visit lone soldiers' home.
5:45pm - Arrive at hotel for check-in.  Enjoy Happy Hour.
8pm - Dinner with Israeli YAD group at Kibbutz Lavi.  Dance troup performance followed by folk dancing with instruction and Israel Bazaar with various artists.

Evening at Kibbutz Lavi Guest House

Click here to see a map of Misgav/Karmiel. 

Click here to learn more about Misgav and Karmiel.

 

Thursday, March 2, 2006                
Break into small groups to see different sights in the area.

7:15am - Breakfast at hotel.
8:30am-1pm - Tour of Misgav in three tracks (each bus will participate in all three activities):

  1. Yodfat archaeology site with Marc Rosenstein (hike)

  2. Israeli-Arab School

  3. Visit to Kibbutz Eshbal (Ethiopian boarding school)

1:30pm-3pm - Lunch in Karmiel at Raymond, Karmiel's new youth club house.  Discussion around Israel and the Galilee.  Opportunity to purchase original artwork.  Plant garden outside youth club.

3:30pm-4pm - Visit to the Karmiel promenade.  See baby bricks.

4pm-5:30pm - Visit to Yad L'Banin and fill Purim baskets for Lone Soldiers.  Meet Lone Soldiers and present them with the baskets.

6pm-9pm - Dinner with host families.

9pm - Optional night out at cub in Karmiel or return to hotel.

 

Evening at Kibbutz Lavi Guest House

 

Friday, March 3, 2006                     

7am - Breakfast at hotel.

7:45am - Buses depart for Jerusalem.

9:30am - Stop to view Separation Fence en route to Jerusalem.

11am - Scavenger Hunt of Jerusalem with Jonty Blackman.

1:30pm - Check in to hotel.

3:30pm - Candle lighting.  Depart hotel for Old City.

5pm - Reflections at the Kotel.

5:30pm - Time at the Kotel. (Option to walk or ride bus back to the hotel)

7pm - Intimate Shabbat Dinner for Pittsburgh delegation in hotel dining room.  Pittsburgh rabbis and Jonty will lead the service.


Evening at Prima Kings Hotel

 

Saturday, March 4, 2006
Optional Shabbat services (list of times and locations will be distributed upon check-in on March 3)

7:30am-10:30am - Breakfast at hotel.

  Option 1: Dead Sea/Masada for an additional fee with Gershon and Reuven

  Option 2: Israel Museum (Open 10am-4pm)

  Option 3: Walking tour of Jerusalem with Jonty and Francis (11am and 4pm).

5pm - Study sessions around 3 topics with Pittsburgh rabbis.

6:15pm - Group will meet up for Havdallah and drinks and will then have a free evening.

 

Evening at Prima Kings Hotel


Sunday, March 5, 2005

8am - Breakfast with Nahman Shai, Director-General of the United Jewish Communities of North America.

9:30am - Depart for Yad Vashem

10:15am - Tour of Yad Vashem

1:30pm - Tour of Latrun (box lunch)

4:30pm - Check in to hotel in Tel Aviv
6:30pm-7:30pm - Happy Hour
8pm-9:15pm - Opening Plenary - Speakers: Gil Sassover, Famous Israeli Actor; Cindy Attias & Steve Marlowe, Tel Aviv One National Chairs; Eden Harel, MTV Europe VJ
9:15pm - Mosaics…Tastes and Sights of Israel
11pm - Party
