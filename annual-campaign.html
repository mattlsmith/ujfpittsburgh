Annual Campaign

About

The Annual Campaign is at the core of what we do, providing essential, unrestricted dollars for the organizations that provide amazing services to our community and to those in need around the world.

The Annual Campaign addresses the most pressing needs and issues at a moment's notice, feeds the hungry, helps the unemployed, supports families with special needs, and funds Jewish education. The Annual Campaign has been the glue that has held the Jewish community together for over 100 years and will continue to do so for at least the next 100.

Today, we celebrate over 100 years of overcoming obstacles and continuing to build a strong, ever-growing Jewish Pittsburgh.

Please, on behalf of the thousands of men, women, and children who are in desperate need of a better life, commit to the Annual Campaign and become part of a more than 100-year-old solution. Through the Jewish Federation, you can make a world of difference and deliver the gift of hope to those in need.

The Annual Report shows where your money goes. Please take a look.
