What is PGFundConnection:
PGFundConnection is a web based record keeping system that the Federation uses to manage transactional performance activity for your funds held with the UJF Foundation. PGFundConnection has been developed in a daily valuation environment, allowing for daily transaction activity and performance valuation.
Login To PGFundConnection

How can I use PGFundConnection?
Through your internet web browser, you will now be able to review your UJF Foundation Fund statements, as well as current fund activity.

How to navigate within PGFundConnection:

In Box/Main Screen
This is the main window of the site. It lists all accounts to which the donor has access.

Fund Summary Icon
This option allows the donor to run an interim statement of his/her account for a specific time frame. (The earliest available date is 10/1/2001).

This will show, in summary and detail, a beginning balance, any transactions in a designated time frame and the ending balance.

Please note if you wish to view current activity:

  • Contributions take 3 business days to settle before they will be reflected in your account.
  • Distributions are processed twice a month (typically around the 10th and 26th of the month.). Any off cycle requests can be made with a fee of $30.00.
  • Current contributions and distribution requests may be in process and may not be reflected in your current fund activity.

How to access Account Summaries:

  • Select the Fund Summary Icon.
  • In the Account number drop down box, select your account.
  • Enter a beginning and ending time frame.
  • Click on Run Report Icon.
  • The report will display on the screen.

With the icons in the top right corner of the screen, you have the option to print the report to a local printer, e-mail the report or send it to excel and save locally on your PC

Options Icon
Allows you to change your password at any time.

  • Select the Options Icon.
  • Select password administration.
  • Enter your existing password.
  • Create your new password (8-40 characters long).
  • Re-enter your password for verification.
  • Notification will show your password has changed.

Statements Icon
Allow you to reference prior statements for your account(s)

  • Select the Statements Icon.
  • A listing of all available accounts and their quarterly statements will show.
  • Double click on the account number for the period you would like to review.
  • Adobe Acrobat will open and a copy of your quarterly report will show. (Adobe Acrobat is needed in this section. A free version is available once you log in.)

Home Icon
Will return you to your inbox from anywhere within the site

Logout Icon
Will log you out of PGFundConnection
