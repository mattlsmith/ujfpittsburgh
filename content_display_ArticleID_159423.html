The Nosh Network

You Don't Know Everybody in Pittsburgh... Yet!

Nosh Network Weekend
See you at our next Nosh Network Weekend.
October 27 - 29, 2006

Want to meet new people? This is a great opportunity to meet new friends in small, intimate settings. Separate get-togethers will be held for singles, couples, families with children, and graduate students. Pot-luck dinner parties and Sunday brunches are hosted in various homes throughout Pittsburgh .  The Nosh Network is open to individuals ages 22-45.

 
Nosh Network Holiday Connection
 
New to town?  Can't make it home for the holidays?  The Nosh Network Holiday Connection will help make a match, so that you can share your holiday observance with others in the Pittsburgh Jewish community.
 
Contact Anne Richardson for more infomation at 412.992.5222 or arichardson@UJFpittsburgh.org.
