Partnership2Gether: Pittsburgh-Karmiel-Misgav

Host Family Survey

Partnership2Gether is a program of the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh supported by the Annual Campaign

 

Welcome to the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's Partnership2Gether program, or "P2G." Partnership2Gether ("P2G") is a sister-city partnership linking the Pittsburgh Jewish community to Karmiel and the Misgav region, in Israel's central Galilee. Coordinated locally by the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh and supported by the Federation's Annual Campaign, since 1995 the partnership has created numerous opportunities to build relationships between Pittsburghers and Israelis. P2G programs are evaluated and approved each year by a joint steering committee consisting of members from Pittsburgh and Karmiel/Misgav.


Thank you for considering serving as a host family for one of our visiting Israeli delegations. This survey must be completed by one adult in the household. When you get to the end of the survey, note that you must enter a series of letters and/or numbers before clicking "Submit" or you will get an error when you try to submit the form. Thanks again for considering being a host! If you have questions about this survey or hosting, please contact the Partnership Coordinator at the Jewish Federation at 412-992-5208.

CARPOOLING

During the group's visit we ask that hosts drop-off their guests in the morning at the designated location in their schedule (often this is the Jewish Community Center), and pick them up in the evening, usually at central location like the Jewish Community Center. If you cannot drop-off and pick-up each day, let the Partnership2Gether Coordinator know or coordinate directly with another host. Occasionally, we need adults to help out with transportation aside from regular drop-offs and pick-ups. Please let us know your availability so we can plan accordingly.
