Community Relations Council (CRC)

The Jewish Delegate Assembly of Greater Pittsburgh passed resolutions recently providing platforms for future advocacy efforts by the Community Relations Council of the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh.

The latest resolutions address Aging and Poverty, LGBT Discrimination, Price Tag Attacks, Genetic Testing, Anti-Semitism in Europe, Gender Violence, Proactive Israel Support & Countering Anti-Jewish and Anti-Israel Activity in Pittsburgh, Food Insecurity and Hunger, and Inclusion and Disability in the Jewish Community.

For more about each resolution, click the links below:

September, 2016: Jewish Delegates Assembly of Greater Pittsburgh Statement on the Constitutional Right to Vote
March, 2016: Stereotypes, Prejudice and Discrimination
March, 2016: Boycott, Divestment and Sanctions Movement against Israel
April, 2015: Genetic Testing and the Jewish Community
April, 2015: Anti-Semitism in Europe
November, 2014: Gender Violence
May, 2014: Senior Poverty
May, 2014: Price Tag Attacks in Israel
May, 2014: LGBT Discrimination
December, 2013: Proactive Israel Support & Countering Anti-Jewish and Anti-Israel Activity in Pittsburgh
December, 2013: Inclusion and Disabilities
December, 2013: Food Insecurity

CRC Code of Conduct
