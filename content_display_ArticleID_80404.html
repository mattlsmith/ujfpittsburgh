Glossary of Terms



Welcome to our Online Jewish Word Glossary. Inside, you'll find words and phrases of both Hebrew and Yiddish origin, covering the range of the Jewish experience, from annual and lifetime events to ritual objects and Jewish cuisine. Reprinted with permission from the Jewish Federation/Jewish United Fund of Metropolitan Chicago
