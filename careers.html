Careers

Since its founding in 1912, the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh has been the catalyst for building and strengthening the Jewish community of Pittsburgh. Through its planning and fund-raising efforts, the Federation has worked to enhance the quality of life for all members of the Jewish community -- in every stage of life, in Pittsburgh, Israel and throughout the world. The Federation builds community through the funding and planning support it provides to its partner agencies. The Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh is one of the largest distributors of community philanthropic funding in Western Pennsylvania.

Frequently included in the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette's yearly list of "Top Work Places" in Pittsburgh, the Federation offers rewarding work and great benefits in a friendly, professional atmosphere. Come grow with us.
