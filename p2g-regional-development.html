P2G - Regional Development

 

Regional development programs take place only in Karmiel/Misgav and are for the economic improvement of the region. Projects have included:

P2K Bike Trail: The Partnership provided seed funding for a bike trail that has increased tourism to the region with several biking competitions that now take place along the P2K Bike Trail.  Click here for a map and more information about the P2K trail.

Moona: A start-up aiming to promote technology education and entrepreneurship in the Galilee region.
