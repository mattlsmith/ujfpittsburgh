A Message from Jeff


So Much to Celebrate

Last night, the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh held its final Board meeting for this term.  We took the opportunity to look back at our accomplishments over the last two years.  We are really proud to have launched our Jewish Community Security Director position and have been impressed with all that he has accomplished in such a short time.

Convsations That Count: Privacy in a Digital Age
June 29, 5:30 PM - 7:00 PM
This men’s event is facilitated by Foundation Scholar Rabbi Danny Schiff and featuring Dr. James "Kip" Currier, JD. We will explore the modern ethical and legal implications of maintaining one's right to privacy and the Jewish perspective on that issue.
