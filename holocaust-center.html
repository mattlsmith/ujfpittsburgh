The Holocaust Center of Pittsburgh is an affiliated organization of the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh. Their mission is to inspire engagement with Holocaust history and to connect it to today.

Established in 1981, the Holocaust Center serves as a comprehensive resource center that helps people learn about the Holocaust and counter intolerance and hate in all its forms.
