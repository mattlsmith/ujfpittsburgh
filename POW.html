POW! Power of Women Volunteer Series

POW! is a volunteering initiative created just for women in the Pittsburgh Jewish community.

POW! provides unique opportunities for hands-on volunteering throughout the city for women who are passionate about their community. Together, we learn about the strength within ourselves and discover the power of Jewish women to make a difference to those in need. POW! is a joint venture of Women's Philanthropy and the Volunteer Center of the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh.  

Register Now! 

Monday, April 18 , 2016
10:00 am - 12:00 noon

Join us at Our Clubhouse as we build care packages for people with cancer, focusing on the mitzvah of helping the sick. We will also learn more about Our Clubhouse, the facility, and its mission to create communities of support for those living with cancer, along with their family and friends. The packages will be donated to local hospitals in Pittsburgh.
