Come learn about the Jewish Federation of Greater Pittsburgh's 5th Annual Volunteer Center Mission to Israel. The City of Karmiel and the Misgav Region have immediate needs for volunteers seeking an adventure that will enrich their lives immeasurably. Karmiel and Misgav are Pittsburgh's partners in Israel in the innovative Partnership2Gether (P2G) project, which builds people-to-people connections between Israelis and Jewish communities in the Diaspora through an array of educational, cultural, and economic initiatives. Join other members of the Pittsburgh Jewish community, March 19th-26th, in northern Israel's magnificent Galilee region, where you will delight in Israel's natural beauty and forge meaningful new friendships. Your service and presence in this area are an invaluable boost to the morale of the Israeli people.
