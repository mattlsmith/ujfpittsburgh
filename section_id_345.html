Shalom Pittsburgh

Shalom Pittsburgh serves the young adult population (ages 22-45) through outreach and engagement programming and relocation services. Whether you are planning a move to Pittsburgh, newly relocated to Pittsburgh or have been here for a long time, Shalom Pittsburgh has something for you.

For more information on any of Shalom Pittsburgh's services, contact Suzanne Green or by phone at 412.992.5222. ...

You're moving to Pittsburgh tomorrow... next week... next month... next year.

Do you need a place to live? Maybe a new job? How about information on schools, synagogues, real estate and restaurants? Need to know where your can get you hair cut? Have clothes tailored?

Shalom Pittsburgh can help! Please take advantage of these no cost services. Contact Suzanne Green or by phone at 412.992.5222.
