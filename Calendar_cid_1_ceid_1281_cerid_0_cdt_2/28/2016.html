Kidz Item Swap and Shop Feb 28 2016

Join Shalom Pittsburgh for

Kidz Swap and Shop

February 28, 2016

JCC Squirrel Hill, Levinson Hall

10:00am - 12:00pm

Cost: $5

per person to drop, swap, and shop (CASH ONLY - PAYABLE AT THE DOOR). All donations benefit the Squirrel Hill Food Pantry. 
Online registration is encouraged.

 

Items for children all the way up to age 10! 

People who donate at least 5 items will be allowed to enter the swap and shop 15 minutes before the general public! Drop off items at the Squirrel Hill JCC between Monday Feb. 22 and Friday Feb. 26 (between 8am and 5pm) to receive your early entry wristband. You MUST have your wristband at the swap and shop in order to enter early. 

What should I bring?

Reusable shopping bags to collect items as you go. Donatables like clothes, books, toys, strollers, swings, etc.* 

*Donated items should be clean and in good condition. We are not responsible for the condition of donated items. Also, please do not bring cribs, they will not be accepted. Left over items will be distributed to a variety of community organizations.
